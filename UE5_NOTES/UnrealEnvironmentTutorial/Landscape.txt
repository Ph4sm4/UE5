landscape technical guide (ue5 docs)

sculpt brush -> level up the terrain using left mouse button, shift + left mouse button, decreases the terrain level

smooth brush -> smoothes the edges of the hills

flatten brush -> flattens the area, making, for example a top of the hill, completely flat, by leveling up the terrain underneath

TO EXPLORE: settings -> plugins -> landmass

manage tab -> you can add/delete landscape tiles
